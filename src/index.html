Here in Sky Lunar Designs, we are obsessed with the customer. Every design is made with strong attention to detail to make sure that it satisfies the customer in every possible way. We are partnered with Printful, a company that is well known for making high quality merchandise at a reasonable price. We primarily design clothing for Dog lovers, but we take requests for any design that you want on a T-shirt. We design merchandise for both men and women. We take pride that our customers feel secure shopping from us. Our online store has all different types of merchandise; such as shirts, sweaters, hoodies, necklaces and other cool accessories.

If for any reason you are unsatisfied with our products, please return the product and we will issue a full refund within 2 business days. We learn from our mistakes and try to improve the customer experience everyday. We hope you enjoy our products and show them off to your friends!

Our number one weakness has always been Golden Retrievers and Labradors. Our Golden Retriever themed shop is sure to envy even the most committed Golden Retriever owners. Most of our shirts have strong messages that clearly demonstrate the love you have for your Golden Retriever. We understand that our pets are also a part of our families, and they can never be replaced! Life is most definitely better with a Retriever, wouldn't you agree? Here at Sky Lunar Designs, we certainly do!

Our prices for our merchandise are very reasonable. Just about anyone can afford our merchandise at our low prices. Please take advantage of these prices!
